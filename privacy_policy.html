Privacy Policy

UnitConvert Pro

Last updated: June 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how UnitConvert Pro ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our Android application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

UnitConvert Pro does not collect any personally identifiable information. We do not require you to create an account.

The following non-personal data may be collected automatically through third-party services:

  • Usage data — app events, feature usage, and session information (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Device information — device type, operating system version, and advertising ID (via Google AdMob)
  • Crash reports — anonymous crash logs to help us improve stability

2. How We Use Information

The data collected through third-party services is used solely for:

  • Understanding how users interact with the app to improve features
  • Displaying relevant advertisements (AdMob)
  • Diagnosing and fixing technical issues

We do not sell, trade, or rent any user data to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

UnitConvert Pro uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase Analytics — anonymous usage analytics
  • Google AdMob — advertisement serving

These services may collect data as described in their respective privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

4. Data Storage

All conversion history, favorites, and preferences are stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party. You can delete this data at any time from the app's Settings → Clear History.

5. Children's Privacy

UnitConvert Pro is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our app, please contact us and we will promptly remove it.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
